My Story

Located in the Dallas/Ft. Worth area, I have been in the Graphic Design industry since 2011. I have worked for companies big and small and had the privilege to work for a huge number of clients in the process.

I graduated from The Art Institute of Houston - North with a B.F.A. in Graphic Design in 2012. I have worked for private businesses and at the corporate level, as well as taking on freelance work whenever I can.
