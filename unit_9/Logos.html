Free to Give Houston and Inuvio

Free to Give Houston is a non-profit company that fights to change legislation in Houston to be able to easily hand out food to homeless people in the city.

Inuvio is a ID and document scanner company that is made for the Medical industry. They streamline data entry for doctor's offices so that staff may better serve the people in the office.

Freedom Munitions and Sister Companies

Freedom Munitions is an ammunition manufacturer based in Houston. They are a 'one stop shop' for ammo and produce on all levels. They make and sell all components of ammo including the bullet itself and the casing and primers.
